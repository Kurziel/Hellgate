    • Update a15 b105.2 (10/9/16)

  • Common zombies can have multiple walk styles , so they are not as predictable based on how they look.
  • Increased the spawn frequency of the a15 UMA zombie types (bikers, hazmat, soldier, etc).
  • Reduced the head shot damage multiplier for most weapons, especially firearms (all weapons still have higher multipliers than vanilla).
  • Increased hp on zombies with helmets, significantly increased those with armor and a helmet.
  • Removed the spider zombie.
  • Screamer zombie now has the traits of the spider zombie (walk style, ability to climb and speed).
  • Feral zombie now has the traits of the spider zombie.
  • Feral zombie should only spawn as part of the hordes.
  • Bloodmoon hordes have been disabled. The graphical effect remains for now, however.
  • Reduced xp from killing zombies. Should now be ~25 standard zombies per lvl to begin. This does not impact xp gained from crafting and skills.
